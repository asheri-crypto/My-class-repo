No person is allowed to cheat in any exam or assignment. Anyone caught cheating will be expelled from the school immediately and may only come back in the next academic year. Cameras are going to be installed in all exam rooms to ensure integrity and honesty.

Every class member must participate in mopping the classroom at least 2 to 3 days per week. Cleanliness is everyone's responsibility, and it keeps our environment healthy and pleasant.
